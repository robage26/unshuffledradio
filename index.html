About the Show

This Is Your
Mixtape.

Unshuffled is a solo-produced radio show about music, memory, and the stories that live inside the songs you forgot you knew.

Every episode is built around a theme — a feeling, a question, a moment in time. Ten songs. Always ten. Every decade represented. One conversation with one listener driving somewhere they needed to go.

The music is sacred. The humor lives everywhere else. And the pauses do the heavy lifting.

The Segments That Make It Unshuffled

🕰️

Time Warp

A year frozen in amber. Three clips — TV, film, ads, news — that put you right back in the room.

📈

Chart Snapshot

Five songs from one year's Billboard Hot 100. The obvious picks and the ones nobody remembers.

🎵

Forgotten Classic

One song that deserved better. No setup. No send-off. Just the music — and a sweeper to close the door.

🎯

5in5 Challenge

Five songs. Five seconds each. Can you name them from the opening alone? The reveal comes at the end.

🎤

Comedy Break

One observational, one absurdist. The host never explains it. Neither should you.

📺

Commercial Flashback

Ads from before you could skip them. 1950s through 1980s. They went to number one too.
