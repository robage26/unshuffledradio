✍ How to Add a Story

Copy one of the story blocks below and paste it above the others. Fill in:

id="story-X" — unique ID for the story (used for direct links)

.story-tag — category label e.g. "Behind The Song" or "Music History"

.story-date — date you wrote it

.story-title — your headline

.story-content — your full article, wrapped in <p> tags

<img class="story-img"> — optional image at top of card

Then in the main site admin, paste the link as: https://robage26.github.io/unshuffledradio/blog.html#story-1
